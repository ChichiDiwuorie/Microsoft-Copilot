## **Top 10 Copilot tips for everyday tasks**

**Summarize lengthy emails:** When faced with a lengthy email chain in Outlook, simply type "Summarize this thread" in the Copilot prompt box or click the "Summarize" button, if available. Copilot will swiftly analyze the entire conversation, distilling the most important points, action items, and decisions into a concise summary. This allows you to grasp the essence of the discussion without having to read through every message, saving you valuable time and mental energy.

**Draft emails:** Banish writer's block and accelerate your email communication with Copilot's intuitive drafting capabilities. Whether you're responding to a client inquiry, sending a project update, or simply reaching out to a colleague, Copilot can help you craft professional and effective emails in seconds. Simply provide a brief prompt outlining the recipient and the email's purpose, such as "Draft an email to Sarah about the upcoming project deadline," and Copilot will generate a comprehensive draft tailored to your needs. This eliminates the need to stare at a blank screen, struggling to find the right words.

**Analyze data in Excel:** Microsoft Copilot transforms your Excel experience by providing instant data analysis and visualization capabilities. Whether you need to identify trends, compare sales figures, or uncover hidden patterns, Copilot can help you decipher your data with just a few natural language prompts. Simply type your request in the Copilot input box, such as "Show me the sales trends over the last year" or "Create a pivot table to compare product performance by region," and Copilot will analyze your data and present the results in a clear and informative format.

**Create PowerPoint presentations:** Microsoft Copilot revolutionizes the presentation creation process by transforming your ideas into visually stunning slides in a matter of seconds. Simply provide a brief prompt describing your desired topic, such as "Create a presentation about the impact of artificial intelligence on the workplace," and Copilot will generate a comprehensive slide deck complete with relevant content, images, and formatting. This eliminates the need to manually design each slide, freeing up your time to focus on crafting a compelling narrative and delivering an impactful presentation.

**Generate meeting notes in Teams**: In the fast-paced world of virtual meetings, keeping track of key discussions, decisions, and action items can be a challenge. Microsoft Copilot steps in as your dedicated meeting assistant, offering real-time summaries and post-meeting recaps to ensure everyone stays on the same page. During a Teams meeting, simply ask Copilot to "Summarize this meeting," and it will diligently capture the most important points, including key takeaways, decisions made, and tasks assigned. This eliminates the need for manual note-taking and allows participants to focus on active engagement and collaboration.

**Rewrite text in Word:** Elevate the clarity and professionalism of your written work with Copilot's expert editing capabilities. Whether you need to condense a lengthy paragraph, rephrase a complex sentence, or simply polish your writing style, Copilot can help you refine your text in Word. Simply highlight the section you want to improve and ask Copilot to "Rewrite this in a more concise way," "Make this sound more professional," or provide any other specific instructions. Copilot will analyze the selected text and suggest revisions that enhance clarity, readability, and overall impact.

**Generate ideas in Word:** Microsoft Copilot can help jumpstart your creativity and generate a wealth of ideas to get you started. Whether you're looking for a specific angle, a unique perspective, or simply some fresh inspiration, Copilot can be your brainstorming partner. In Word, simply type "Give me some ideas for a blog post about [your topic]" and Copilot will analyze your prompt and generate a list of potential ideas, each with a brief description to help you visualize the possibilities.

**Translate text in Word:** Microsoft Copilot eliminates language barriers with its seamless translation capabilities. Whether you need to understand a foreign-language email, translate a technical document, or simply communicate with someone who speaks a different language, Copilot is your multilingual companion. In Word, highlight the text you want to translate and instruct Copilot to "Translate this into [desired language]." Within seconds, Copilot will provide you with an accurate and contextually relevant translation, making cross-language communication effortless and efficient.

**Create to-do lists in OneNote:** Staying organized and on top of project tasks can be a daunting challenge, but Microsoft Copilot simplifies the process by generating comprehensive to-do lists in OneNote. Whether you're starting a new project or need to revamp your existing task list, Copilot can help you create a structured and actionable plan. Simply tell Copilot "Create a to-do list for [project name]," and it will leverage its understanding of project management principles to generate a list of relevant tasks, milestones, and deadlines. This saves you the time and effort of brainstorming tasks from scratch and ensures that you haven't overlooked any crucial steps.

**Find information in Edge:** In the vast landscape of the internet, finding relevant information quickly and efficiently can be a daunting task. Microsoft Copilot in Edge acts as your personal research assistant, providing instant answers and summaries to your questions, right within your browser. Whether you need to clarify a concept on the current webpage, research a broader topic, or simply get a quick overview of the content you're reading, Copilot has you covered. Simply activate Copilot in the Edge sidebar and ask your question in natural language. Copilot will leverage its vast knowledge base and advanced language processing capabilities to provide you with accurate and concise responses, saving you the time and effort of scouring multiple sources.
